About

Designing experiences
that linger.

I graduated from the Game Art & Design program at SeamEdu School of ProExpressionism, Pune — and since then, I've been driven by a single conviction: games have the power to transport players to extraordinary worlds, evoke real emotions, and tell stories that stay long after the screen goes dark.

I work at the intersection of game design, level design, and narrative writing. Whether I'm shaping a world's systems or the voice of its characters, I bring a player-first mindset and an eye for detail that makes every interaction feel intentional.

"Polished, bug-free gameplay is not just a technical goal — it's a form of respect for the player."
