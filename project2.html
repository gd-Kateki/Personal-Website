Overview

A fractured psyche.

Deep Into Dreams is a first-person psychological horror game that explores the fractured psyche of a protagonist trapped within their own escalating nightmares. The gameplay revolves around environmental storytelling, puzzle-solving, and tension management, eschewing traditional combat for a mechanics-driven vulnerability.

Project Details

Core Pillars.

  • Genre: Psychological Horror / Narrative Walking Simulator / Puzzle
  • Platform: PC / Console
  • Art Style: Surreal Realism (shifting from crisp, sterile environments to distorted, organic, and decaying dreamscapes).
  • Core Pillar: Vulnerability. The player cannot fight back; they must hide, outsmart, or endure the manifestations of their trauma.
The Lore

Trapped in the subconscious.

The game takes place entirely within the subconscious mind of Dr. Aris Vance, a sleep researcher who attempted to use an experimental neuro-sync device to cure his own chronic, grief-induced insomnia. Instead, the machine malfunctioned (or perhaps succeeded too well), locking him in a cascading loop of his deepest regrets, fears, and repressed memories.

Each "level" of the game represents a deeper layer of his subconscious, twisting his real-world memories into horrific, distorted obstacles.

The Cast

Manifestations of trauma.

Dr. Aris Vance
Protagonist
A grieving sleep researcher trapped within his own malfunctioning neuro-sync device, forced to navigate shifting night terrors where facing his repressed memories is the only way to rewrite his waking reality.
The Weaver
Antagonist / Stalker Entity
A shifting, porcelain-masked manifestation of Aris's medical fixation and crushing guilt, relentlessly hunting him to ensure he remains permanently locked within the cascading loop of the dream.
Lily Vance
The Anchor
The spectral presence of Aris’s deceased eight-year-old daughter, appearing through fleeting shadows and glowing childhood drawings to guide him deeper into the subconscious toward acceptance.
